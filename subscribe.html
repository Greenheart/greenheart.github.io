How does this work?

You can use a feed reader (sometimes called an RSS reader) to follow multiple blogs or websites via their web feeds. This is a convenient and privacy-friendly way to stay up to date without subscribing to email newsletters or using relying on social media algorithms.

Get started

  1. Read this short intro to how feeds work.
  2. Install and get started with a feed reader for your device. I recommend Feeder (Android) and NetNewsWire (iOS + macOS). There are also email clients Thunderbird (desktop + mobile), and even browser extensions where you can subscribe to your feeds.
  3. To add feeds from the blogs and websites you want to follow, by copy the feed URL into your reader. You can often use the landing page URL, like https://samuelplumppu.se and the feed reader will find the feed automatically. You can also use the feed icon to find direct links to add in your reader.
